About Us

Trusted Healthcare Since 2005

Founded in 2005 by Dr. S.K. Jain, Jain Hospital has been serving Shahjahanpur with reliable and compassionate medical care for over two decades. We bring together advanced medical expertise and modern facilities to support families through every stage of life — from fertility and maternity to child and dental care. Our hospital is recognized for its Advanced IVF and Gynaecology Centre, expert pediatric services, modern orthodontic and dental treatments, and a fully equipped in-house pathology lab.
